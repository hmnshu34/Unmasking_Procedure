13 July 1858
“Clergyman’s Salaries”
Some of the papers, great and large, are making a great ado about clerical pay. It appears that Beecher makes $12,000 a year and Taylor of Grace Church receives $10,000, and Tyng, Bellows, Osgood, Cheever and other leading clergymen make from three to six thousand. Well, what of it? A clergyman’s life is one of the most laborious and impossible imaginable, and if some of them are well paid so much the better. But ___ ___ that these brilliant examples save but ___. The rank and file of the army, especially in the country, find it, hard work to get along at all on salaries averaging $400 per annum.
