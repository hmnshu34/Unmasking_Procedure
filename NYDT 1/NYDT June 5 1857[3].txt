	It is easy to see that, by following up the line of argumentation introduced by the Chief Justice, the number of persons capable of being citizens would be very considerably reduced. To negroes and Catholics we should have to add all the free-thinkers—a class which, since the Bible has been set up as the bulwark of Slavery, rum-selling and polygamy, has rapidly increased—and all the Universalists—a fast increasing sect; while the list of the excluded would have to be adorned not merely with the names of a Dred Scott and a Taney, but with those of a Franklin, a Jefferson, a Starr King and a Chapin.
