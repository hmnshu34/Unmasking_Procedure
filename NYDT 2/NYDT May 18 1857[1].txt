	Such an event must necessarily be followed by serious modifications of the Mormon polity. That the entire delusion would be extinguished by it is not probably; but while the Church would natu-rally be split into several opposing fragments, many of its more intelligent and uncorrupted members would be restored to their senses and to civilized life. We shall wait with interest for further intel-ligence from the Territory, and meanwhile we do not doubt that the President will see the necessity of hastening the departure of the troops intended for that quarter. We trust, also, that an energetic and judicious may at once be sent out with them to assume the office of Governor.
